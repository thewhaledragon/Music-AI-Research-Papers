# 1. Music Generation (Generative Music AI)

The most popular modern area.

### 1.1 Symbolic Music Generation

Works with **MIDI / notes / score representations**.

Subtopics

* Melody generation
* Harmony & chord progression generation
* Multi-track generation
* Style transfer
* Counterpoint generation
* Lead sheet generation
* Arrangement generation

Typical models

* LSTM
* Transformer
* Music Transformer
* VAE
* Diffusion

---

### 1.2 Audio Music Generation

Direct waveform generation.

Subtopics

* Text-to-music
* Prompt-based music generation
* Diffusion audio synthesis
* Neural audio synthesis
* Style-conditioned generation

Examples

* MusicGen
* AudioLDM
* Riffusion

---

### 1.3 Lyrics Generation

Subtopics

* lyric generation
* rap generation
* melody-conditioned lyrics
* multimodal lyric + melody

### 1.4 Interactive / Co-creative Music

Human + AI composition systems.

Subtopics

* AI accompaniment
